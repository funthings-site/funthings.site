Detailed Privacy Policy

1. Information We Don't Collect

We are proud to say that FunThings.site does not collect, store, or process any personal information from our users. This includes:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Usage patterns or browsing history
  • Device information or browser details
  • Any data generated by our tools

2. How Our Tools Work

All our random generators and fun tools operate entirely within your web browser. This means:

  • No data is sent to our servers
  • All calculations happen locally on your device
  • Results are not stored or transmitted anywhere
  • Your privacy is completely protected

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use minimal local storage for:

  • Theme preference: To remember if you prefer light or dark mode
  • No tracking cookies: We don't use any tracking or analytics cookies
  • No third-party cookies: We don't set any third-party cookies

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Web hosting: To serve our website content
  • Content delivery: To ensure fast loading times
  • No analytics: We don't use Google Analytics or similar tracking services

5. Contact Information

If you contact us via email (mredstone31@gmail.com), we will:

  • Use your email address only to respond to your inquiry
  • Not share your email with third parties
  • Not add you to any mailing lists without permission
  • Delete your email after responding (unless you request otherwise)

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is safe for users of all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no special considerations for children's privacy. Parents can feel confident that their children can use our tools safely.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last modified" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at mredstone31@gmail.com.
