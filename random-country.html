Amazing Country Facts

🌍 Total Countries

There are 195 countries in the world today, including 193 UN member states and 2 observer states (Vatican City and Palestine).

🏔️ Largest Country

Russia is the largest country by land area, covering about 17.1 million square kilometers (6.6 million square miles).

🏝️ Smallest Country

Vatican City is the smallest country in the world, covering only 0.17 square miles (0.44 square kilometers).

🗣️ Most Languages

Papua New Guinea has the most languages of any country, with over 800 different languages spoken!

🌊 Most Coastline

Canada has the longest coastline in the world, stretching over 202,080 kilometers (125,567 miles).

🏜️ Driest Place

The Atacama Desert in Chile is considered the driest place on Earth, with some areas receiving no rainfall for years.
